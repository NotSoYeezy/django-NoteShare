About NoteShare

NoteShare is a web application that I made with Django, to add something to my github portfolio, was my first bigger app that I’ve created. Working on it was a great journey for me as a beginner django developer. Every time I wanted to implement a functionality, I was learning a new concept, thing or technology. It took me approximately 6 months to make this app look as I wanted it to look. Of course, it’s not perfect, It definitely has some bugs - bigger or smaller, however I hope that it will be forgiven (because of my lack of experience). If you want to follow my programming journey, come to my GitHub❤️.
